What We Do

We build AI systems that healthcare organizations can trust — engineered for clinical accuracy, protected by design, and compliant by default.

Predictive Analytics & Forecasting

Turn historical patient and operational data into clinical foresight. We build ML models for readmission risk, patient deterioration, population health stratification, and revenue forecasting — deployed directly where your clinicians and administrators work.

AI Agents & Integrations

We design and deploy autonomous AI agents that act inside your existing clinical systems — handling triage, prior authorization, appointment scheduling, documentation, and cross-system data orchestration. Purpose-built integrations with EHR, EMR, and health data platforms.

Model Fine-Tuning

General-purpose LLMs don’t understand your clinical context. We fine-tune foundation models on your organization’s proprietary health data — producing models that speak your specialty, reflect your protocols, and perform on your patient population.

Health Data Protection

End-to-end protection for Protected Health Information. We implement HIPAA-compliant data pipelines, PHI de-identification, encryption at rest and in transit, and comprehensive audit trails across every system that touches patient data.

Clinical NLP

We extract structured intelligence from unstructured clinical text — EHR notes, discharge summaries, radiology reports, and medical records — using NLP models purpose-built for healthcare language and terminology.

Compliance & AI Governance

We embed regulatory compliance into every layer of your AI system. From HIPAA and HITRUST to SOC 2 and emerging AI governance standards — every model we deliver is auditable, explainable, and defensible.
